Something a little mor concrete

My name is Annathina. I have eight siblings, of which I am the youngest, save one. As of fall 2016, I am officially a student of Bob Jones University. I am majoring in creative writing because I love to create. The written word,the printed page, they give me so much opportunity to create worlds of my own. That being said,punctuation is my enemy. Also: numbers make no sense to me.

After starting school, I joined a society, this year, and for the frest of my college career, I hope to play intermural soccer with the rest of my society team. I don't claim to be good, but I have a lot of fun. I love to read, so I used to spend a lot of my free time at the local library. Now that I'm a college student, I never have free time.
